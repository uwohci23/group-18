The system remains consistent both internally and externally. Formatting and color choices are very similar between the different screens of the application. For semiotics, we chose conventionally used icons that are instantly recognizable. Furthermore, the game controls follow the standard for their respective genres. These choices allow the user to transfer their knowledge from previous digital experiences. That being said, the internal consistency could be further improved by making the gameplay canvases more similar to one another in terms of color schemes, sizing and positioning.


Internal Consistency:
  • Buttons and color choices are consistent throughout all pages.
  • Pause/game over screens are formatted the same within each game.
  • Game-specific menus follow the same layout and feel.
External Consistency:
  • Back button, which returns to the previous screen, is a conventional arrow positioned at the top left.
  • Instructions can be exited using an 'X', which follows the standard for most pop-up menus.
  • Within games, we chose to include a conventional pause icon.
  • For snake/tetris, the typical controls are present (arrows/wasd). For minesweeper, conventional right and left click functionality is present.
