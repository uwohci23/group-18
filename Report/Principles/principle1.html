Landing Page and Main Interface
  • Metaphor:
    The landing page viusals with an arcade machine in the center depicting graphic elements from all the 3 games and the sound track playing in the backgroud of the main interface all reinforce the 'virtual arcade' metaphor to users.
  • Affordance:
    Reduce complexity and depict elements as they are perceived with immediate response, the home page buttons have added hover effects to show that they are clickable.
  • Fitt's Law:
    Large, prominent buttons are placed in the middle of the screen in order to make them quickly and easily accessible.
